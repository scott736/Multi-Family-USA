## Comparison framing

Use this framework to compare cost of capital, proceeds certainty, and operational flexibility between the two options.

## Decision criteria

1. Does the structure support your business plan timeline?
2. Can NOI support refinance assumptions under downside rates?
3. Are recourse and covenant terms aligned with risk tolerance?
