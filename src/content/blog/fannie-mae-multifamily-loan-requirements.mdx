## Fannie Mae multifamily requirements in context

Fannie Mae multifamily financing serves **stabilized US apartment buildings** with five or more units meeting agency underwriting grids. Requirements center on normalized NOI, coverage metrics, asset quality, and sponsor capacity—not residential borrower income tests.

## Core underwriting metrics

Agency sizing uses the tightest of:

- **DSCR** on normalized in-place NOI (directionally ~1.20x–1.30x for many stabilized deals)
- **Debt yield** floors that vary by market and product
- **LTV** caps tied to appraisal and grid

## Stabilization and occupancy expectations

Agency lenders favor durable physical and economic occupancy with credible rent roll support. Transitional lease-up stories usually belong in **bridge** execution until stabilized metrics are achievable.

## Sponsorship and legal structure

Expect guarantor financials, entity documentation, and experience narrative—especially on smaller sponsor teams or emerging markets. SPE covenants and post-close reporting are standard.
