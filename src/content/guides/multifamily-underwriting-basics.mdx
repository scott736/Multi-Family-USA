## Why this matters

This guide provides practical underwriting direction for commercial multifamily financing. The objective is to improve execution quality before term sheet collection.

## Core framework

1. Define realistic NOI under stabilized operations.
2. Size debt using DSCR, debt yield, and leverage guardrails.
3. Stress test downside occupancy and refinance outcomes.
4. Align hold period and business plan with lender product design.

## Common mistakes

- Overstating rents or understating operating expenses.
- Ignoring extension and refinance conditions on bridge debt.
- Selecting capital based on headline rate without execution risk context.
