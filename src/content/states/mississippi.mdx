## Mississippi multifamily financing snapshot

Mississippi supports multiple multifamily execution paths for 5+ unit properties, but leverage and pricing vary by asset quality, sponsor track record, and market depth. Use this page for directional underwriting context before lender outreach.

## What lenders focus on in Mississippi

- Stabilized DSCR and debt yield under current rates
- Submarket rent durability and tenant demand depth
- Insurance and tax pressure on break-even occupancy
- Exit optionality to agency, bank, or debt-fund refinance

## Practical underwriting notes

Anchor your base case using conservative rent assumptions, normalized operating expenses, and a reserve-aware capital stack. If your plan requires rapid NOI expansion, pre-negotiate extension paths and refinance triggers before close.
