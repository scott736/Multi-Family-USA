## Asset overview

Large Suburban Apartment Communities assets require tailored underwriting assumptions around operating ratios, turnover, and leasing velocity.

## Financing takeaways

- Match loan structure to renovation and stabilization path.
- Validate downside occupancy coverage and refinance exit levels.
- Align reserve assumptions with property age and capex plan.
